FAQs

Can I meet with the team before committing to services?

Since we see our patients at home, we are happy to set up a phone conference or virtual meeting if you’d like.

How long is therapy needed for your patients?

This is determined by therapists based on medical necessity on a case-by-case basis. Typically our patients are seen by therapists for about 6-8 weeks, but this can vary.

Would patients qualify for continued therapy?

Patients qualify for continued therapy based on medical necessity.

How do you determine what types of therapy a patient needs?

We work with facility staff and family members who refer a patient to therapy for a specific reason following a particular incident or change in status. Based on clinical decision making skills, our therapists can determine if the patient could benefit from other disciplines based on a patient’s strengths and deficit areas.

What are your office hours?

Therapists treat patients from 7AM to 5PM Monday through Friday, but they can make adjustments based on patient preference and availability.  If you are looking to reach office staff, feel free to reach out any time and someone will get back to you within 24 business hours.

How far in advance do I need to make an appointment?

An initial evaluation takes about 1-2 weeks to get scheduled based on therapist availability. Once the initial evaluation is completed, treatments are automatically scheduled with the therapist based on the frequency they set.

How are your services different from services at an outpatient clinic?

Our therapists are able to treat patients in their own environment whether that be at an assisted living facility, or in their own home. All of our therapists are Dementia Care Specialists and focus on caring for the older adult population. Therapy at Home therapists use a collaborative approach to include patients, assisted living nursing, family, MDs, and other therapists to maximize carryover and understanding for everyone involved in a patient’s care.

How do you help patients with goal setting?

Therapists set goals based on their initial assessment in collaboration with the patient’s important functional and personal goals. Patients and therapists work towards these goals during therapy and can be adjusted and updated based on an individual’s progress.

What service area(s) do you service?

Greendale, Greenfield, Franklin, Muskego, Brookfield, Elm Grove, Wauwatosa, Cudahy, St. Francis, South Milwaukee, and Oak Creek.
Exceptions to the service area can potentially be made with additional cost.
