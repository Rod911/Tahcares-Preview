Welcome to Therapy at Home!


Therapy at Home offers physical therapy services by experienced professionals both at Assisted Living Homes as well as in the Comfort of Your Home. Based on the patient’s needs, we will create a plan for restoring functionality. Most importantly, we strongly believe in care that makes fitness fun and doable.


TAH is a mobile physical therapy practice geared toward rehabilitation at the comfort of your home. After working as a home health physical therapist for several years, I saw a need for focused and individualized, holistic care to improve the quality of life for my patients. I understand the challenges of the aging population and focus my treatment on maintaining the patient’s independence by offering personable, family-based health and wellness. I welcome you to explore and like my Facebook page and be in touch if you have any questions regarding the services we offer. Thank you all for your support!
