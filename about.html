Our Story

One Patient Inspired a New Approach

Therapy at Home Founder Muddassir Mohiuddin was touched by the life of one patient. This patient affected him so profoundly that he was inspired to touch the lives of others every day. In his own words, hear the story that drove him to make independence for seniors his personal mission:

It takes only one story to impact your life. This story is about a client of mine, Shirley. Shirley was in her late 80’s, living independently in her home. She had lived in her home for 63 years. This home held many memories for her: caring for her parents, getting married, her dogs, and raising a family. She was motivated to continue living at her preferred home.

When I started to care for her, she could barely get around the house. Her medical situation was complex; she was dealing with multiple health conditions. She had difficulty moving around the house and caring for herself. At this point, she was unsure of her lifelong goal to age in place, amongst her memories. She was worried about her lifestyle, her culture, her memories, her motivations, and her home. She knew no one would care for her as much as she would, even though and it was time to take some action.

At this point, I made her goal my goal. All that mattered was to help her continue to live at home safely. It took a collaborative effort to achieve this goal. She just did not get by in a wheelchair, but returned to walking around the house, regaining the ability to enjoy her backyard and beyond. She thrived beyond her home and return to the things she loved: dining out with family, being part of the bigger community at large and celebrating the holidays with family.

Dr. Mohiuddin’s experience with Shirley became the foundation for Therapy at Home. He built a collaborative physical therapy practice that helps seniors age independently, and achieve the goals that matter to them most.

A story of Recovery… Prevention ….Wellness so she could thrive at her home

About Therapy at Home

Therapy at Home is a comprehensive therapy private practice in the Greater Milwaukee area (located in Greendale, Wisconsin) that emphasizes recovery, prevention and wellness. We offer a full range of therapy services, including: occupational, speech, massage, and physical therapy... all available in the comfort of your home. 

We seek to improve the quality of life for patients and their families through physical therapy, as well as occupational and speech therapy. More importantly, we want to help promote healthy aging to prevent further measures like the move to a skilled care facility.

Physical Therapy That Gets You Back to Your Life

Many times, an event like an accident or an injury can lead to you needing physical therapy. What these events have in common is that they impact your quality of life, and we are here to help you get back to your baseline. We understand these challenges, and our goal is to not only help you achieve your goals, but to enhance them, so you can continue to thrive independently.

For Independent Adults

Skilled Expertise to Help You Live the Way You Want

You deserve care that goes above and beyond minimum expectations. Therapy at Home strives to provide care that is not only life altering, but life changing.

At Therapy at Home, we understand the importance of independence. Preventative wellness is crucial to helping you maintain your independent lifestyle. We enable you to be more connected, engaging with the people and activities you love. We work with you to develop the skills and habits you will need to age healthfully, and continue to choose the home and life you want.

For Families

Successful Aging in Place

Here at Therapy at Home ™ , we understand the unique needs of every individual and create a tailored plan to achieve personal goals related to successful living in place. Our collaborative team approach and specialized treatment for aging adults and memory care expertise allows us to work with patients and their families to build plans for success based on goals that matter most to each individual, such as: traveling, beginning or resuming hobbies, enjoying leisure activities, playing with grand or great-grandchildren, and attending important life events.

Skilled Expertise

Our Therapy at Home service providers bring a wide range of experience, and many have dedicated their lives to aging care, completing specialized certifications and education on caring for seniors. Beyond that, however, we want to not only serve aging adults, we want to do it right.

We are committed to simplifying the process and bringing the difference that matters at home with our clinical expertise and knowledge. Many therapy practices make this promise, but we also place highest priority on listening to the patients we serve and their families.
